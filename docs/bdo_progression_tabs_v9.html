Items Only Obtainable From Gathering

These items either only come from gathering or are primarily generated through gathering activities.

Item Use Why It Matters
Hard Black Crystal Shard Enhancement materials Used to craft Concentrated Magical Black Stones for enhancing boss gear.
Sharp Black Crystal Shard Enhancement materials Same purpose as Hard shards but for weapon upgrades.
Caphras Stones (small amounts) Gear progression Occasionally obtained from gathering; needed for late-game gear upgrades.
Spirit Dust Caphras crafting Used to craft Caphras Stones through processing.
Fairy Powder Fairy growth Used to upgrade fairy tiers and reroll fairy skills.
Traces (Trace of Earth, Savagery, etc.) Alchemy Required for many alchemy recipes and lifeskill progression.

Should You Gather?

  • Not required for combat progression
  • Most items can be purchased on the market
  • Gathering becomes valuable mainly for lifeskill-focused players

Since your main focus is combat grinding and AFK fishing, gathering can remain optional unless you later pursue lifeskill progression.
